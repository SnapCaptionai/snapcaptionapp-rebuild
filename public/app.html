How To Use SnapCaption 📖
Never used AI before? No problem. We got you covered step by step.
🤖
What is AI?
AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. Think of it like a very smart assistant that has read millions of books, articles, and social media posts. When you describe what you need, it writes something brand new just for you — in seconds. You do not need to know how it works. Just type what you want and press the button.
✍️
Step 1 — Caption Generator
This tool writes captions for your social media posts.
HOW TO USE IT:
1️⃣ Tap Caption in the bottom menu
2️⃣ Tap your platform — Facebook, TikTok, Instagram etc.
3️⃣ In the text box, describe your photo or video in plain English
   "Sunset photo at the beach with my kids, feeling grateful"
4️⃣ Pick a tone — Casual, Funny, Inspiring etc.
5️⃣ Tap Generate Captions
6️⃣ Read the options, tap Copy on the one you like
7️⃣ Paste it into your post — done! ✅
🎣
Step 2 — Hook Writer
A hook is the very first line of your video. It decides whether people keep watching or scroll past. This tool writes 6 options for you.
HOW TO USE IT:
1️⃣ Tap Script in the bottom menu then Hook Writer
2️⃣ Describe what your video is about
   "I'm sharing 3 ways I make money from home as a mom"
3️⃣ Pick a Hook Style — Curiosity, Bold, Story etc.
4️⃣ Tap Write My Hooks
5️⃣ Pick the one that feels most like you
6️⃣ Say that line at the very start of your video
🎬
Step 3 — Script Writer
This writes your entire video word for word. Hook, intro, main points, product mention, and call to action — all done for you.
HOW TO USE IT:
1️⃣ Tap Script in the bottom menu
2️⃣ Choose your platform and video length
3️⃣ Describe what your video is about
4️⃣ Choose your style — try Realization + Explanation + Example for deep content
5️⃣ Add your product in the Product Mention section (optional)
6️⃣ Tap Write My Script
7️⃣ Read straight from your phone while filming or memorize sections
#️⃣
Step 4 — Hashtag Generator
Hashtags help new people find your content. This tool creates 3 sets — broad reach, targeted, and niche — so you get the best mix.
HOW TO USE IT:
1️⃣ Describe your content in a few words
2️⃣ Pick your niche (Lifestyle, Parenting, Business etc.)
3️⃣ Tap Generate — copy all 3 sets
4️⃣ Paste them at the end of your caption or in the first comment
🔁
Step 5 — Repurpose Tool
Write once, post everywhere. Paste one caption or idea and this tool rewrites it for TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn and Twitter — all at once.
PRO TIP:
Write your best Facebook post first — then repurpose it. 6 platforms in one tap. That is the creator cheat code. 🔑
💡
The Golden Rule of AI
The more detail you give, the better the result. Instead of "motivational post" try "motivational post about not giving up when building a business, speaking to moms who feel overwhelmed". The AI matches your energy. Give it something to work with and it will blow your mind.
